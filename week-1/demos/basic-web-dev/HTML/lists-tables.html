Grilled Salmon Recipe

Ingredients

  • Salmon Fillet, approx. 3lbs, skin on side
  • Olive Oil
  • Your Favorite Seasonings

Cooking Instructions

  1. On a cutting board that you can clean easily, slice salmon fillet into approx 8 equal sized pieces
  2. Preheat grill to 400 degrees
  3. Pour a small amount of olive oil on each piece of salmon
  4. Add on as much seasonings as desired
  5. Cook on grill for approx 12-14 minutes, starting skin side down, flipping halfway through

Nutrition Facts

Protein 34g
